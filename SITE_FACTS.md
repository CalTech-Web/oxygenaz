## FAQ Content

**Q: What is Oxygen Wellness & Physical Therapy?**
A wellness recovery center offering non-invasive, science-backed therapies designed to reduce pain, improve recovery, enhance circulation, and promote overall well-being.

**Q: Who benefits from your treatments?**
Athletes, injury recovery patients, chronic pain sufferers, those managing inflammation or autoimmune conditions, and anyone focused on proactive wellness.

**Q: Can friends participate in my session?**
Yes, friends are welcome to join for core services like cryotherapy and infrared sauna.

**Q: Do you accept insurance?**
Services are not billed through insurance. However, HSA and FSA accounts typically cover our sessions. We provide receipts for reimbursement.

**Q: Do I need a prescription or referral?**
No prescription or referral is necessary. All services are available to everyone.

**Q: Do you accept walk-ins?**
Core services accept walk-ins during business hours. Physical therapy and additional services require advance booking.

**Q: How do I book an appointment?**
Schedule online through our website or mobile app, or call us directly at 602.888.7992.
